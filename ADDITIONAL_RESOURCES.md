## üîÑ Continuous Learning Strategy

### Daily Habits (15-30 minutes)
- Read AWS/DevOps blogs and news
- Practice command line operations
- Review documentation for tools you use
- Participate in online communities

### Weekly Goals (2-3 hours)
- Complete hands-on labs or tutorials
- Work on personal projects
- Watch technical presentations or webinars
- Contribute to open source projects

### Monthly Objectives
- Learn a new tool or technology
- Complete a certification study guide chapter
- Attend virtual meetups or conferences
- Update your professional portfolio

### Quarterly Milestones
- Take a certification exam
- Complete a significant project
- Present at a local meetup
- Mentor someone learning DevOps

Remember: The key to mastering DevOps is consistent practice and staying current with rapidly evolving technologies. Focus on understanding concepts deeply rather than just memorizing commands.
